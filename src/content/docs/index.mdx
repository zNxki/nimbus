## Why Nimbus?

<CardGrid stagger>
	<Card title="Fast setup" icon="rocket">
		One-liner install, guided Google Drive auth, and you're backing up in under two minutes.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Smart excludes" icon="approve-check">
		Global and per-directory exclude rules — ignore `node_modules` in one project, keep it in another.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Set & forget" icon="clock">
		Schedule automatic backups with `nimbus each 6h` and let cron do the rest.
	</Card>
	<Card title="No lock-in" icon="open-book">
		Built on top of [rclone](https://rclone.org/) — your files stay in a plain Google Drive folder, not a proprietary format.
	</Card>
</CardGrid>

## Quick install

```bash
bash <(curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/zNxki/nimbus/main/install.sh)
```

Works on **Ubuntu/Debian** (apt), **Arch** (pacman), and **Fedora** (dnf) — the installer detects your package manager automatically.
